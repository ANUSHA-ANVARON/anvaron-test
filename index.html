UrbanEase – Smart Parking & Mobility Platform
UrbanEase is our flagship platform, built to transform how parking and urban mobility are managed in fast-growing Indian cities.
Commuters
with real-time access to verified parking spots, EV charging points, and on-route fuel stations
Parking operators
with digital tools like QR-based access, staff-side apps, and smart dashboards
Cities and infrastructure partners
with data-driven insights to reduce congestion, emissions, and inefficiencies
From ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) to live availability, digital payments, and operator-level dashboards, UrbanEase brings together modern technology and urban intelligence to build cleaner, more organized mobility systems. Currently live across Hyderabad and Telangana, we are rapidly scaling to other metro regions including Bangalore and Mumbai — helping shape the future of connected, sustainable movement in India.
